#DraftComplete 
- Produced by [[Gonads|gonads]] and [[Adrenal Gland|adrenal gland]] in the [[Cell Organization|smooth endoplasmic reticulum]]
- Molecular structure similar to [[Cholesterol synthesis|cholesterol]]
	- They are thus [[Polarity|nonpolar]] and [[Membrane Structure|can enter the cell]]
- Bind to intracellular or intranuclear receptors and form a **hormone-receptor complex**
	- Translocation: actin
- Once inside the nucleus, they bind to DNA and cause [[Gene Regulation|gene regulation]]

#### Types of Steroid Hormones
- [[Public/Endocrine System/HPA Axis|Glucocorticoids]]
	- Cortisol/cortisone
	- Prednisone
- Mineralocorticoids
	- [[Public/Endocrine System/Renin-Angiotenisin-Aldosterone System|Aldosterone]]
- Androgens
- [[Menstrual Cycle|Estrogens and Progestens]]
